Frequently Asked Questions

Below you will find answers to questions we get asked most.

Still have questions? Contact us: support@elepha.io

Why should I use your platform for recruiting?

We offer a unique solution for your recruitment teams. Automated phone calls to five or 50,000 job applicants to check interest in a role, deliver a custom message and collect up-to-date information from interested candidates. Allows recruiters to focus only on few potential candidates, rather than many irrelevant job applicants.

How much do you charge?

Here's the good news - we're cheaper than your in-house recruiting coordinators cost. We charge per job applicant in our outreach, our pricing ranges between ₹8 to ₹12 and includes cost of calls, sms, email, data collection and follow-up drip campaigns.

What kind of roles can be hired using this platform?

While the platform works for all roles, its just a platform for your communication with job applicants. However, you will make the most value for ongoing roles. Say you want to fill tens or hundreds of Software Engineers, we can ensure there is a continuous pipeline available for you to tap into.

How long will the calling process take?

It depends on the number of job applicants one has to reach-out to. We can take, upto 20,000 calls per hour, i.e. 200,000 in a day. Need more? We will be happy to help, Contact support@elepha.io

Do all the call get dialled from the same number?

Yes, each client is assigned a unique number and all calls will display this number. The company can run any number of campaigns and all campaigns will display this unique company number.

What happens when the job applicant calls back on the same number?

All calls landing (incoming) on the company number will have a standard message, something like, Thank you for calling the recruitment Division of ABC Company, press 1 for career opportunities etc.., At the end of the call a custom message is delivered to the caller and if the caller is already in the system, meaning the caller is responding to a campaign, will receive the message as per the campaign assigned and a new caller will receive generic company message.

Can I display the unique number on my job postings and email signatures?

Yes, the number assigned to your company can be displayed on email signatures and job postings. When an incoming call is received, a unique company message will be played and a message will be delivered basis the user inputs.
