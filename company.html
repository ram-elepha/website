We Have a Dream !

We started in 2009 as a recruitment agency, delivering world-class recruitment solutions and services. Having spent many years hiring for technology companies, we realised recruiting is a challenge in every company big or small.

We believe that in this socially connected world, all the talent that a company wants to hire are all accessible online and possibly interested in a role change. The challenge is to reach out to each individual and present the company for them to consider the job role.

An Automation that Works:

Elepha, started as an in-house technology to help cater to our growing recruitment services business. We knew a recruiterâ€™s success is mostly determined by the number of calls made to job applicants, the more the number of calls, the more successful is a recruiter.

By building tools to automate phone calls to hundreds of job applicants to check interest in a role, deliver a custom message and collect up-to-date information from interested candidates, allows recruiters to focus only on few potential candidates, rather than many irrelevant job applicants.

Also, scheduling of job interviews can best be achieved by automating the call, delivering a message, checking for the best time slots and closing the loop with an automatic job interview invite.

Elepha, the Digital Assistance for Recruiters is built by a group of Recruiters and Engineers, who realised that a simpler solution exists in the right automation. We hope for a world where finding talent can be Simpler, Better and Faster.
